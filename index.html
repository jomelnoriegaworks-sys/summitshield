Why Now

Life insurance gets more expensive every birthday. And every check-up.

Rates are based on your age and health today. Waiting a year can add 8–12% to your monthly premium for the exact same coverage. A single new diagnosis — high blood pressure, a borderline blood test — can double it or take some carriers off the table entirely. The best time to lock in a rate is the version of you that exists right now.
