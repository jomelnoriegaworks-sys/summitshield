Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

What we collect

When you request a quote, book a consultation, or contact us, we collect the information you choose to share — typically your name, email address, and details about the coverage you're exploring (such as household size, coverage amounts, or property information).

How we use it

  • To prepare quotes and coverage recommendations for you
  • To schedule and conduct your consultation
  • To submit applications to carriers you choose to apply with
  • To check in about your policy — such as your annual coverage review

What we never do

We never sell your information. We never share your details with other agents or marketing lists. Submitting a quote request here will not trigger calls from third parties — the only people who will ever contact you are Summit Shield advisors.

Sharing with carriers

If you decide to apply for a policy, we share your application information with the insurance carrier you selected — that's a necessary part of underwriting. Carriers handle your data under their own privacy policies, which we'll point you to before you apply.

Data retention & your rights

We keep client records for as long as needed to service your policy and meet insurance record-keeping regulations. You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (where regulation allows) at any time.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at summitshieldinsure@gmail.com and we'll respond the same business day.
